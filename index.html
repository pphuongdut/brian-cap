USERS GUIDE

Bridge Eth to Base

You will need a DeFi Crypto wallet to begin with. The preferred wallet is to use is the Coinbase wallet, however if you already have a Metamask or Trustwallet, etc then that will be fine. You can send Eth directly from your Coinbase CEX account to your DeFi wallet, or you can use the Base bridge linked below.

Download CB Wallet Base Official Bridge
Buy $BCAP on Uniswap

The $BCAP contract address is listed above. You will need to import this token into Uniswap by copying and pasting the contract address into the search bar after you have clicked "Buy". You can then swap Base Eth for $BCAP Tokens.

Add $BCAP token to wallet

If you use MetaMask, click "import token" and paste the $BCAP contract address listed above into the field provided. This will then show your $BCAP tokens inside your wallet!
